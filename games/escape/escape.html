Reflection

What is this project?

The Memory Museum Escape Room was an Independent Study I participated in with five other students where we created a functional escape room that we presented to the public.

My role

I was the lead puzzle designer and producer for this project, but I also contributed to the narrative aspects of the room and played one of the characters.

What I accomplished

By the end of this project we successfully created and ran a functional escape room for over 100 students, faculty and members of the public.

What I Learned

As a group, we struggled most with organization. For example group members had very different schedules and levels of commitment. It was difficult to pull everything together in such a short amount of time. I learned a lot about what role/roles I am good at in a team environment. In this case I was reluctantly thrust into a leadership role and my hesitation to embrace that role had a negative outcome on the project as a whole. During this project I became more confident in performing in leadership roles and I learned how to balance having multiple roles in a small, creative team.
