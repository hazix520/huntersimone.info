Arena Dodgeball

Project Type: Personal Project

Game Type: Video Game

Software: Unity, Visual Studio

Coding Languages: C#

Arena Dodgeball is currently in the prototype stage. The focus of this prototype was primarily on the movement. Players can hold up to two dodgeballs that they can charge to throw. Players can also wall jump if they jump while touching a wall at the peak of their jump. Skilled players can drop a dodgeball below their feet to act as another jump.

  • Physics-based Movement System
  • Pickup and Inventory System
  • Limited Multiplayer (using Netcode for GameObjects package
