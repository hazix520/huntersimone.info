Flippin Floppers

Project Type: Game Jam

Game Type: Video Game

Software: Unity, Visual Studio

Coding Languages: C#

Flippin Floppers was made in 2 days for the 91st Mini Jam on itch.io. The optional theme was UFO and the required limitation was "fish as a mechanic." I decided to make a game where the fish are the flying objects. In this game you move your buckets to catch the flying fish. Catching using the brown bucket lures more fish to the area, the blue bucket makes the next fish very big and the green bucket sells the fish. The goal is to make enough money to buy a fishing boat
